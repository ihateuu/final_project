She traverses the Void, surviving from the depths. Kai'Sa is a deadly and elegant warrior, combining the Void and humanity, fighting to survive and striking back to avoid being consumed.

Background Story

Kai'Sa was once a mortal who was swallowed by the Void. After years, she miraculously survived and returned. Wearing armor symbiotic with the Void, she moves swiftly and lethally. Though many fear her, she knows only she can resist the Void's corruption.

Abilities

Passive - Second Skin

Her basic attacks apply Plasma, dealing bonus magic damage.

Q - Icathian Rain

Fires up to 12 missiles that evenly distribute among nearby enemies, dealing physical damage.

W - Void Seeker

Fires a long-range energy blast that deals magic damage and applies Plasma to the first enemy hit.

E - Supercharge

Briefly gains a burst of movement speed and then rapidly increases attack speed.

R - Killer Instinct

Dashes near an enemy champion and gains a shield.
